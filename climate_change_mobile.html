🔥 California Wildfires

In early 2025, Southern California experienced a number of fires with historic amounts of damage totaling over $250 billion and over 57,000 acres of land.

🌊 Venice Flooding

Rising sea levels are flooding Venice regularly, threatening its future. In November of 2019, Venice experienced a string of nonstop floods for a week.

🌡 Middle East Heatwaves

Currently, temperatures in some areas exceed 50°C, disrupting life and increasing health risks.

🌊 Rising Sea Levels

Sea levels are rising due to melting glaciers and thermal expansion, threatening coastlines globally.

🌾 Horn of Africa Drought

From 2020–2023, severe drought affected Somalia, Ethiopia, and Kenya with devastating results.

🐠 Coral Bleaching

Marine heatwaves are bleaching coral reefs, especially the Great Barrier Reef, threatening marine biodiversity.
